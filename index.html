Our Story

Crafting Timepieces Since 2023

Founded by Anusha Thapa in the heart of Thimphu, Bhutan, Swift Watches was born from a passion for precision and a love for timeless design. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create watches that are not just timekeeping devices but wearable art.

Each Swift watch is meticulously crafted by skilled artisans who pour their expertise and attention to detail into every component. We source only the finest materials from around the world to ensure durability, comfort, and elegance.

Inspired by the relentless march of time and the beauty of Bhutan's landscapes, our designs reflect both modern sophistication and traditional craftsmanship.

About Swift Watches

Discover the passion, craftsmanship, and vision behind our timepieces

Meet Anusha Thapa

Hello! I'm Anusha Thapa, the founder and creative force behind Swift Watches. My journey in horology began as a childhood fascination with my grandfather's pocket watch, which he brought back from his travels.

After studying design in Thimphu and working with several luxury brands, I realized there was a need for timepieces that combined Swiss precision with Bhutanese aesthetics at accessible prices. In 2023, I decided to turn this vision into reality.

Every Swift watch is designed with purpose and passion. We work closely with master watchmakers who have decades of experience to ensure each component meets our exacting standards.

When I'm not designing new collections, you can find me hiking in the beautiful mountains of Bhutan, where I draw inspiration from nature's timeless beauty.

Our Workshop

Nestled in the heart of Thimphu, our workshop is where magic happens. Here, skilled artisans with decades of experience assemble each watch by hand, paying meticulous attention to every detail.

We've created a space that blends traditional watchmaking techniques with modern technology. Our state-of-the-art equipment allows for precision that would be impossible to achieve by hand alone, while our craftsmen ensure each watch has that human touch.

Quality control is rigorous at every stage. Each movement is tested for accuracy, each case inspected for imperfections, and each strap checked for comfort and durability before it leaves our workshop.

We invite you to visit us and see the craftsmanship firsthand. There's nothing quite like watching a timepiece come to life.

Our Services

Beyond selling watches, we provide comprehensive care for your timepieces

Watch Care Services

Professional Repairs

Our certified watchmakers can handle everything from simple battery replacements to complex movement repairs. We use only manufacturer-approved parts and tools.

Services include: Battery replacement, movement servicing, crystal replacement, crown/stem repair, water resistance testing

Turnaround time: 3-7 days depending on complexity

Deep Cleaning

Restore your watch's original luster with our professional cleaning service. We use ultrasonic technology to remove dirt from even the hardest-to-reach areas.

Process includes: Case and bracelet disassembly, ultrasonic cleaning, polishing (upon request), reassembly and lubrication

Turnaround time: 2-3 days

Custom Engraving

Make your watch truly unique with our precision engraving service. Perfect for gifts, anniversaries, or personal statements.

Options include: Monograms, dates, special messages, custom designs (consultation required)

Turnaround time: 1-2 days for standard engraving

Vintage Restoration

Bring new life to your heirloom timepieces with our vintage watch restoration service. We handle each piece with the care and respect it deserves.

Services include: Movement overhaul, dial restoration, case refinishing, strap/bracelet restoration

Turnaround time: Varies based on condition (consultation required)

Authentication

Not sure about your watch's authenticity? Our experts can verify the genuineness of your timepiece and provide a certificate of authentication.

Process includes: Visual inspection, movement examination, comparison with manufacturer specifications, documentation review

Turnaround time: 1-3 days

Regular Maintenance

Preventative care is the best way to ensure your watch lasts for generations. Our maintenance plans keep your timepiece in optimal condition.

Includes: Movement lubrication, gasket replacement, water resistance testing, timing adjustment, general inspection

Recommended: Every 3-5 years depending on usage
